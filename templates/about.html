OpenWindows

“OpenWindows is a web application whose purpose is to help in the specification of glazing and ventilation for residences in England and Wales. Based on noise levels outside a dwelling and the elements of the façade, it calculates the internal noise levels. It then compares those levels them to guideline levels in Professional Planning and Guidance (ProPG). It is intended to be used by design teams, local authorities and specifiers of glazing and ventilation. “
