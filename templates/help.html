Help for entry fields

One can enter up to four types of noise levels which the facade is exposed to.

  • The energy "average" noise levels over a 16 hour daytime period (LAeq16h)
  • The energy "average" noise levels over an 8 hour night-time period (LAeq8h)
  • The 10 highest impulsive noise level of an 8 hour night-time period (LAeq8h)
  • The 1highest impulsive noise level of an 8 hour night-time period (LAeq8h)
You can enter the noise level at the facade as an overall level in the left hand entry box or the spectra at each octave band from 125 Hz to 2000 Hz, sepearated by "-" or "/"


Enter either
  • The volume in cubic metres (m3) OR
  • The area (m3) x height (m) OR
  • 3 dimensions (length (m) x width (m) x height (m) )


From drop-down list enter the type of element you wish to include on the facade.


Enter the area of facade of the element or equivalent area for ventilation elements.


If this element is placed on a quieter facade than enter the number of deciBels quiter.


Filter out elements that do not inclide keywords entered here.
